30+ luxurious color palettes To Inspire Your Branding (2024)

Squarespace Tutorials
May 5
Written By Kseniia Krasilich

I've been a fan of colors for as long as I can remember. From crayons and coloring books as a child to Adobe Illustrator as an adult, colors have always captured my interest. The expression (and often times emotion) colors evoke are something that has interested me since I can remember. Understanding the relationships between colors and their psychology within the context of branding will help you create a uniform look for your brand — regardless of the style.

Finding the perfect color palette for your brand can be a bit tricky. You may think you know the perfect colors for your brand, but do they communicate what you are trying to say? Are they repeated on your website? Do your image files support the colors you chose?

    1. Gold & Burnt Orange color palette

    The gold and burnt orange colors are most frequently found in luxury brands that endear their customers with respect, security and quality. The idea of “high class” is more often associated with this color palette, as both colors are known to be rich and luxurious.

    Website design using Gold & Burnt Orange color palette

     

    2. Royal Blue color palette

    Royal Blue is a luxurious, deep, yet vibrant color. It is powerful and stunning and will make you feel like royalty. This color palette is great for luxury brands looking to make a statement, or companies looking to portray their high-end services.

    Website design using Royal Blue color palette

     

    3. Brown Luxury color palette

    Brown is a warm and welcoming color that is ideal for any business looking to bring in customers. This brown luxury color palette contains a variety of different shades and hues that can be used individually or combined to create unique designs.

     

    Website design using Brown luxury color palette

     

    4. Ivory & Dusty Rose color palette

    The Ivory & Dusty Rose color palette is a light and neutral combination of calming tones that can feel classic, or fresh in their pairing. This palette is ideal for those who are looking to create a brand that embodies elegance, while still remaining simple and approachable. Mixing the rich muted tones of classic ivory with soft dusty rose creates an alluring contrast that results in a rich but understated color scheme.

     

    Website design using Ivory & Dusty Rose color palette

     

    5. Cream & Gold color palette

    A gorgeous combination of creamy gold and white will give your branding a touch of luxury. Your choice of color should be based on the use case for your sweatshirt, but for business purposes, white is more professional. This palette works well for any business that needs to look high-end.

    Website design using Cream & Gold color palette

     

    6. Crimson & White color palette

    Crimson & White color palette for your brand is a modern, bright and luxurious color scheme. Just like the red wine, ivory and white colors speaks of luxury and creates a feeling of warmth.

    Website design using Crimson & White color palette

    This classic combination of rich reds and crisp whites will reinforce your brand's message of luxury, sophistication, and strength, while still being friendly and approachable.

    7. Black & salmon color palette

    Black & salmon color palette is an elegant and luxurious combination. Black looks sophisticated when combined with salmon, which creates an unexpected pop of color. The combination would be perfect for luxury brands that want to show off their unique personality but remain on trend.

    Website design using Black & salmon color palette

    Black and Salmon color palette is in vogue, but not overly feminine. It's a fun and unexpected color combination that's perfect for modern brands that need something more than black and white.

    8. Black & Gray color palette

    Black & Gray is a bold and confident color palette that gives the impression of luxury, success, power, and elegance. This clean design will make your branding stand out from the crowd and create a sense of authority in your audience.

    Website design using Black & Gray color palette

    The monochromatic design helps to create a strong, bold presence yet still creates an understated elegance that represents an expensive brand. This color scheme works well for almost any kind of business website design including financial, law firms and more.

     

    9. Champagne & Black color palette

    A combination of champagne and black is a unique color choice that can inspire your next branding project. This color combination combines the elegance and sophistication of white with the boldness and strength of black for a chic, sophisticated look.

    Website design using Champagne & Black color palette

    This color combination is used to create a sophisticated and stylish look that gives the feel of luxury. The champagne glass is light in weight, airy and usually covered with a thin dimple pattern, making it appear glossy.

     

    10. Khaki & Yellow color palette

    Khaki and yellow color combination is very subtle and attracts more attention. To inspire your website branding design, you can try this simple color combination together. It's also a good idea to use this concept in your everyday life and help attract more people towards your brand.

     

    Website design using Khaki & Yellow color palette

    The Khaki & yellow color combination inspires modern design and freshness. It’s a subtle mixture of energetic yellow with a hint of green, which makes it perfect for a company that aims to attract modern customers.

    11. Dusty Rose & Cream color palette

    Dusty Rose & Cream is a stunning color combination that lets your brand's message stand out, while looking fresh and modern. Pairing dusty rose with cream is not only beautiful, but also gives you lots of options to complement your design.

     

    Website design using Dusty Rose & Cream color palette

    Our Dusty Rose & Cream color combination will inspire you to create a warm and welcoming website that gives your customers the feeling that they are part of an intimate community. Choose from this beautiful color scheme to help you create a welcoming brand identity that's as unique as your business.

     

    12. Ivory & Tan color palette

    Ivory and tan colors blend perfectly in a palette of business sophistication. The perfect colors to boost your branding and marketing strategies by using a color combination that is not just easy on the eye, but also provides a sense of warmth and elegance.

     

    Website design using Ivory & Tan color palette

    The soft, neutral hues of Ivory and Tan are refreshing in their simplicity. This color combination is both elegant and timeless, allowing it to work well for businesses with a wide range of products or services.

     
     

    luxurious color palettes To Inspire Your Branding

    A good color palette will do wonders for your brand, but it's also important to incorporate all of the other aspects of your brand in order to unify your look. Your website, social media channels, logos, graphics, and everything else should fall in line with your color palette. What's more—it should be applied consistently across all of these mediums. That's no small task, but it will pay off in the long run when you are able to say that you have a distinct look that matches who you are as a business and organization.
